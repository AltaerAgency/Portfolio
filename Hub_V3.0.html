Our Audiences// 01

Our Audiences

Core Demographic

The City of Lafayette communicates with and serves everyone who lives, works, visits, and does business here. Our community members each play a role in shaping our City’s identity and future.

As a public service organization, we are committed to transparent, inclusive, and accessible engagement that reflects the diversity of our community.

Personality// 04

Personality

Vibe and Spirit

A unique blend of artsy and outdoorsy. Friendly, neighborly, and diverse, with a down-to-earth sensibility. We strive to communicate like the neighbor you actually want to talk to: approachable, authentic, and welcoming.

Voice Pillars

Engaging, creative, and big-thinking yet approachable. Inclusive and reflective of our eclectic community.

Writing Do's// 05

Writing Do's

City Protocol

Use clear, concise, and plain language. Be conversational yet professional. Lead with what matters most. Use visual elements that reinforce the City’s brand. Always link to official City resources for details and transparency

Digital Presence

Keep social media posts short, clear and impactful. When communicating directly with residents, use “Lafayette” instead of “the City” to sound more approachable. Include clear calls to action when appropriate.

Perspective// 06 + 07

Perspective

First Person (Preferred for community engagement)

Use first person (“we,” “our”) for casual, community-oriented communication. This approach feels welcoming and conversational. Best for social media and event invitations.

"We're excited to invite you to our next town hall"
Third Person (More formal and official)

Use third person for formal, official, or record-based communication. Best for news flashes, press releases, reports and official documents, Council communications and policy or educational content.

"The City of Lafayette has published the 2024 budget."

Brand Imagery // 09

Brand Imagery

Lafayette’s story is told through its people, places, and creative energy. The following image categories guide how we visually represent our community in a way that feels real, vibrant, and distinctly Lafayette.

Access Photo Library
Category 01: People

Authentic, diverse and candid community members that reflects real life in Lafayette. Avoid staged scenarios or generic stock imagery.

Category 02: Scenery

Breathtaking views of the Flatirons and wide-open spaces that define the Lafayette landscape.

Category 03: Events
